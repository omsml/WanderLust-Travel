Who We Are

More Than Just a
Travel Agency

WanderLust Travel was born from a simple belief: that travel has the power to transform lives. Founded in 2006 by a group of avid explorers, we've grown from a small boutique agency to one of India's most trusted travel brands — without ever losing our personal touch.

We don't just book holidays. We craft experiences that become the stories you tell for years. From spontaneous solo adventures to meticulously planned family vacations, every trip we design carries the same DNA: authenticity, care, and a deep love for this beautiful world.
